About Me

I’m Chris Milbourn Pereira, a Computer Engineering student at Fr. Agnel’s College, Bandra. Currently the Media director of Rotaract club of Fr.CRCE, Parent-Rotary Club of Bombay Bandra R.I.D 3141 - Zone 1

I’m passionate about technology, creativity, and personal growth. Alongside my studies, I’m skilled in video editing, photography, and have been learning music for over 10 years. I’m also active in sports and believe in maintaining a healthy balance between work, creativity, and play. Outside of that, I love listening to music, spending time with my cat, playing videogames and travelling with friends — moments that keep me inspired and grounded.
